# thermodynamic-modeling-pipeline
Computational workflow for modeling amino acid formation in Titan’s Selk crater melt pools. Combines Gibbs free energy estimation, polynomial thermodynamic fits, and Cantera equilibrium simulations to study prebiotic chemistry relevant to astrobiology and the Dragonfly mission. Reproducibility dataset archived on Zenodo.

Follow the instructions in the Workflow_Instructions.PDF to begin and then refer to main paper/SI for details https://iopscience.iop.org/article/10.3847/PSJ/ae1c18/

## Repository Structure

data/
    Gibbs energy datasets used in thermodynamic calculations

notebooks/
    Jupyter notebooks for polynomial fitting and equilibrium modeling

inputs/
    YAML templates for Cantera equilibrium simulations

scripts/
    Gibbs free energy estimation tools
